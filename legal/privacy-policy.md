# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 24, 2026

Cyberia Space ("we", "us", "our") operates the Cyberia Space chat application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

> **Note:** This is a draft. Have a legal professional review it before going live.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information you provide directly

- **Phone number:** Used for account authentication via SMS verification code.
- **Handle (username):** The name you choose to display in chats.
- **Avatar color:** A color identifier for your profile.
- **Messages:** Text messages you send in rooms and direct messages.

### Information collected automatically

- **Session data:** Login timestamps, platform type (web, iOS, Android), and session tokens.
- **Usage data:** Rooms you join, messages you send, and general interaction patterns.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To authenticate your account and maintain your session.
- To deliver messages between users in real time.
- To notify you of direct messages when you are not actively using the Service.
- To enforce rate limits and prevent abuse.
- To clean up expired data (messages, guest accounts, inactive sessions).

## 3. Data Retention

- **Messages:** Room messages and direct messages are automatically deleted after 24 hours.
- **Notifications:** Expire after 90 days.
- **Sessions:** Expire after 30 days of inactivity.
- **Guest accounts:** Automatically deleted after 24 hours.
- **Account data:** Retained until you delete your account or we remove inactive accounts.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

- As required by law or legal process.
- To protect the rights, safety, or property of Cyberia Space, our users, or the public.
- With service providers who assist in operating the Service (e.g., Twilio for SMS delivery), bound by contractual obligations to protect your data.

## 5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including:

- Encrypted data in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
- Rate limiting on authentication and message sending.
- Input sanitization on all user-generated content.
- Automatic expiration of sensitive data (OTP codes, sessions).

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate personal data.
- **Delete** your account and associated data.
- **Export** your data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete it.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the Service and updating the "Last updated" date.
